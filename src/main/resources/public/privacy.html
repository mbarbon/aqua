Privacy Policy

Short version

This is a personal project, for fun and learning; the site and application use analytics software to collect anonymous usage data. This data is not sold to third parties or linked to your anime watching preferences.

Enjoy!

If you require additional information about this privacy policy, you are welcome to contact me by e-mail at mattia@aqua-recommend.net.

Collected data:

While you use of the Aqua website or application, some data may be collected, either directly or through a third party. Example of collected data is the anonymized form of your IP address and general information about the device you are using (for example the hardware model, screen size and operating system version).

Data usage:

The data is collected as a learning experience, to analyze web site/application usage in an useful way.

Sharing your information:

The collected information is not shared with any third party other than the ones providing the collection service itself (see "Analytics" and "Crash Reporting" below)

Analytics:

I collect anonymous usage statistics for the Aqua website and application, primarily as a learning experience for me in how to collect and analyze this data in an useful way, and also to get insights on how they are used and how they can be improved. This information is not linked to your MAL account (if you chose to enter it).

Further information on "Google Analytics" and "Google Analytics for Firebase" use of data can be found on the site "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps", (located at http://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/).

Crash reporting:

I collect anonymous crash and malfunction information for the Aqua Mobile application using Fabric's Crashlytics. The data is used to correct issues, and to improve the application.

Crashlytics also tracks your basic usage of the Aqua Mobile application. This information is not personally identifiable and is used by me to improve the crash and malfunction data I collect.
